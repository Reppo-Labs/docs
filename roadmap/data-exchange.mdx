Reppo Data Exchange is the marketplace for discovering, packaging, and monetizing AI training data produced through Reppo. It sits downstream from datanets and gives buyers a venue to access continuously updated, market-curated datasets.

## What it does

Data Exchange gives the ecosystem a place to:

- Discover live datanets and their output.
- Package data into usable products.
- Monetize access, licensing, and distribution.

Participants can price data in stablecoins or other assets, while network-level fees can still flow back into the REPPO economy.

## Why it matters

Datanets create supply. Data Exchange turns that supply into a buyer-facing marketplace, which makes monetization a native part of the network instead of an off-platform side channel.
